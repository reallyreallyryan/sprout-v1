The Origin Story

I wanted to build something tangible. Not another AI wrapped tool in the cloud but something with a body, a purpose, and a job. That's where Sprout came from.

I started with a basil seed, a leaking reservoir, and no formal training. And now? It waters itself!

My journey began when I noticed my plants wilting while I got lost in projects. But instead of just setting reminders, I thought: 'what if the plant could take care of itself?'

So here it is. A robot that lives with the plant and helps it thrive.
